It's that time of year again! TEDxGunnHighSchool will be hosting its tenth annual conference on Friday, January 17, 2020 in Gunn High School's Spangenberg Theatre. This year's theme is New Perspectives.

Theme

One Step Forward Each year, TEDxGunnHighSchool aims to center its annual conference's content around a central theme. Previous themes have included Be The Variable, More Than A Number, Trailblazers, Crossroads, and New Perspectives. Inspired by the vision of challenging the norm and viewing established and novel problems under a new light, we selected One Step Forward as this year’s theme.

With our distinguished set of speakers and performers, we hope to provide a conference experience where the audience will explore innovative ways on how they can take one step forward to a better future.
More information will be announced the closer we get to the conference, including the day's schedule, speakers, and performers!
