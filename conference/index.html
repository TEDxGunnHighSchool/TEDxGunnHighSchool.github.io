It's that time of year again! TEDxGunnHighSchool will be hosting its eleventh annual conference on Monday, February 8th, 2020 in Gunn High School's Spangenberg Theatre. This year's theme is One Step Forward.

Theme

Redefining Normal Each year, TEDxGunnHighSchool aims to center its annual conference's content around a central theme. Previous themes have included Be The Variable, More Than A Number, Trailblazers, Crossroads, New Perspectives, and One Step Forward. Inspired by the vision of challenging the norm and viewing established and novel problems under a new light, we selected One Step Forward as this year’s theme.

With our distinguished set of speakers and performers, we hope to provide a conference experience where the audience will explore innovative ways on how they can take one step forward to a better future.

Come back later for a detailed schedule and descriptions of speakers and performers.
